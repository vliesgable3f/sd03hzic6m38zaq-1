<p><strong>The Electron Microscope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electron Microscopy (SEM)</li><li>Transmission Electron Microscopy (TEM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electron microscope market is primarily divided into two types: Scanning Electron Microscopy (SEM) and Transmission Electron Microscopy (TEM). SEM offers detailed surface morphology and composition analysis by scanning samples with focused electron beams, making it ideal for material science and biology. In contrast, TEM provides high-resolution images of internal structures by transmitting electrons through thin samples, allowing for intricate atomic-level insights. Both techniques are crucial for advanced research applications across various industries including semiconductors, materials, and life sciences.</p></p>
