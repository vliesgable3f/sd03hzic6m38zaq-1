<p><ul><li>Cow's Milk Based Formula</li><li>Soy-Based Formula</li><li>Protein Hydrolysate Based Formula</li><li>Specialty Formula</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infant milk powder market comprises several key types tailored to meet diverse nutritional needs. Cow's milk-based formula, the most common, mimics breast milk's composition. Soy-based formula is suitable for infants with lactose intolerance or cow's milk allergies. Protein hydrolysate-based formula is designed for allergic infants, offering pre-digested proteins for easier absorption. Specialty formulas target specific medical conditions or dietary restrictions, providing tailored nutrition for infants with unique health challenges, ensuring all infants receive appropriate nourishment.</p></p>
