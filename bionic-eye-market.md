<p><ul><li>Electronic</li><li>Mechanical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bionic eye market is broadly categorized into electronic and mechanical segments. Electronic bionic eyes use advanced technology to convert visual information into electrical signals, stimulating the retina or visual pathways to restore partial vision. These devices often involve camera systems and neural interfaces. In contrast, mechanical bionic eyes typically refer to prosthetic devices that replicate eye movement and function without actual visual processing, relying on physical mechanisms to mimic natural eye behavior. Both types aim to enhance visual experiences for users.</p></p>
