<p><strong>The Metal 3D Printer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Selective Laser Melting (SLM)</li><li>Electronic Beam Melting (EBM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Metal 3D printer market encompasses various technologies, including Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electron Beam Melting (EBM). SLM utilizes high-powered lasers to selectively melt and fuse metal powder layers together, producing intricate and dense parts, making it ideal for complex geometries. In contrast, EBM employs an electron beam in a vacuum to melt metal powder, offering high build speeds and reducing thermal distortion. Both methods are widely used in aerospace, automotive, and medical sectors for advanced manufacturing solutions.</p></p>
